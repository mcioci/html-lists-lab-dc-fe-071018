Best kinds of cheese to use in a grilled cheese

  • Pepperjack
  • Aged Cheddar
  • Gouda
  • Brie
  • Colby

How to make a grilled cheese sandwich

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Butter two pieces of bread
  3. Put desired type of cheese in between the bread
  4. Put the sandwich, buttered side down, into the pan
  5. Allow to cook until golden brown
